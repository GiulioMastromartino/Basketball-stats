# Plays Management

The Plays module allows you to manage your team's playbook and associate plays with game events for deeper analysis.

## Overview

The system comes pre-loaded with **65+ plays** from the standard 2025-2026 playbook, including:
*   **Offense**: Pick and Roll, Isolation, Horns, Motion Offense, etc.
*   **Defense**: Man-to-Man, 2-3 Zone, Full Court Press, etc.
*   **Special**: Inbound plays (BLOB/SLOB), Time-out plays (ATO).

## Managing Plays

### Viewing the Playbook
1.  Click **Plays** in the sidebar navigation.
2.  Use the filter buttons (All, Offense, Defense, Special) to narrow down the view.
3.  Click any play card to view its full details.

### Adding a New Play
1.  On the Plays page, click **Add New Play**.
2.  **Name**: Enter a unique name for the play.
3.  **Type**: Select the category (Offense/Defense/Special).
4.  **Description**: detailed explanation of the execution.
5.  **Diagram**: Upload an image (PNG, JPG) of the play diagram.

### Editing/Deleting
*   **Edit**: Update descriptions or diagrams as your playbook evolves.
*   **Delete**: Remove plays that are no longer used. *Note: Deleting a play may affect historical stats if that play was tagged in past games.*

## Using Plays in Games

When tracking a live game or editing game stats:

1.  **Tagging**: When recording a shot, you can select the associated play.
2.  **Analysis**: The system tracks:
    *   **Frequency**: How often a play is run.
    *   **Efficiency**: Points per Possession (PPP) and FG% for each play.
3.  **Reporting**: Generate PDF reports to see which plays are working best against specific opponents.
