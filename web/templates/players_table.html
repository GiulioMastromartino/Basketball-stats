Statistics Glossary

Definitions and formulas for advanced metrics used in the table above.

EFF Efficiency
Standard NBA composite efficiency rating derived from basic box score statistics.
(PTS + REB + AST + STL + BLK) - ((FGA - FGM) + (FTA - FTM) + TO)
ORtg Offensive Rating
Estimate of points produced (scored or assisted) per 100 possessions. Independent of pace.
(Points Produced / Possessions) * 100
TS% True Shooting %
Shooting efficiency that accounts for field goals, 3-pointers, and free throws.
PTS / (2 * (FGA + 0.44 * FTA))
eFG% Effective FG %
Adjusts field goal percentage to account for the fact that 3-point shots are worth more.
(FGM + 0.5 * 3PM) / FGA
Usage %
Estimate of the percentage of team plays used by a player while they were on the floor.
(Player Poss / Team Poss) * 100
Possessions
Estimated number of possessions based on box score data.
FGA + 0.44 * FTA + TOV - OREB
