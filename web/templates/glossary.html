Stats Glossary

Comprehensive definitions for all statistics used in HoopsStats

Traditional Stats

MIN - Minutes Played

Total time spent on the court during the game, shown in MM:SS format.

PTS - Points

Total points scored through field goals and free throws.

REB - Total Rebounds

Sum of offensive and defensive rebounds.

OREB - Offensive Rebounds

Rebounds gathered on the offensive end after a missed shot by your team.

DREB - Defensive Rebounds

Rebounds gathered on the defensive end after a missed shot by opponents.

AST - Assists

Passes that directly lead to a made basket by a teammate.

STL - Steals

Turnovers forced by taking the ball away from an opponent.

BLK - Blocks

Shots deflected or stopped by the defense.

TOV - Turnovers

Loss of possession through errors, violations, or opponent's steals.

PF - Personal Fouls

Illegal physical contact with an opponent. Five fouls results in disqualification.

Shooting Stats

FG% - Field Goal Percentage

Formula: FGM / FGA × 100

Percentage of all field goal attempts (2PT + 3PT) that are made.

2P% - Two-Point Percentage

Formula: 2PM / 2PA × 100

Percentage of two-point field goal attempts that are made.

3P% - Three-Point Percentage

Formula: 3PM / 3PA × 100

Percentage of three-point attempts that are made.

FT% - Free Throw Percentage

Formula: FTM / FTA × 100

Percentage of free throw attempts that are made.

M/A - Made/Attempted

Field goals made out of field goals attempted (FGM/FGA).

FTA/FGA - Free Throw Rate

Formula: FTA / FGA × 100

How often a player gets to the free throw line relative to their shot attempts. Higher values indicate a player who draws fouls frequently.

Advanced Efficiency

EFF - Efficiency

Formula: (PTS + REB + AST + STL + BLK) − ((FGA − FGM) + (FTA − FTM) + TOV)

Box-score based efficiency metric that rewards positive contributions and penalizes missed shots and turnovers.

TS% - True Shooting Percentage

Formula: PTS / (2 × (FGA + 0.44 × FTA)) × 100

Overall scoring efficiency that accounts for 3-pointers and free throws. League average is typically around 53-56%.

eFG% - Effective Field Goal Percentage

Formula: (FGM + 0.5 × 3PM) / FGA × 100

Field goal percentage adjusted for the fact that 3-point shots are worth 50% more than 2-point shots.

GmSc - Game Score

Formula: PTS + 0.4×FGM − 0.7×FGA − 0.4×(FTA−FTM) + 0.7×OREB + 0.3×DREB + STL + 0.7×AST + 0.7×BLK − 0.4×PF − TOV

John Hollinger's single-game performance metric. Average is around 8-10, excellent is 20+.

Offensive Metrics

ORtg - Offensive Rating

Formula: (Points / Possessions) × 100

Points produced per 100 possessions. Elite scorers have ORtg > 115.

PPP - Points Per Possession

Formula: Points / Possessions

Scoring efficiency per possession. Values above 1.1 are excellent, below 0.8 are poor.

USG% - Usage Percentage

Formula: (FGA + 0.44 × FTA + TOV) / Team Possessions × 100

Percentage of team possessions used by a player when on the court. High usage (>25%) indicates a primary offensive option.

A/TO - Assist-to-Turnover Ratio

Formula: AST / TOV

Measures ball security and playmaking. Ratios above 2.0 are excellent; below 1.0 indicates poor decision-making.

Multi-Game Averages

GP - Games Played

Total number of games in which the player has recorded stats.

MPG - Minutes Per Game

Average minutes played per game.

PPG - Points Per Game

Average points scored per game.

RPG - Rebounds Per Game

Average total rebounds per game.

APG - Assists Per Game

Average assists per game.

SPG - Steals Per Game

Average steals per game.

BPG - Blocks Per Game

Average blocks per game.

TOPG - Turnovers Per Game

Average turnovers per game.

CV - Consistency (Coefficient of Variation)

Formula: (Standard Deviation / Mean) × 100

Measures scoring consistency across games. Lower values indicate more consistent performance. CV < 30% is excellent consistency.

Possession Calculation

Formula: Possessions = FGA + 0.44 × FTA − OREB + TOV

Estimates the number of possessions a player uses. This is the foundation for advanced metrics like ORtg, PPP, and USG%.

  • FGA: Each shot attempt uses a possession
  • 0.44 × FTA: Estimated possessions ending in free throws (not all FTA end possessions)
  • − OREB: Offensive rebounds extend the possession, so they don't count as new possessions
  • + TOV: Turnovers end possessions

Performance Benchmarks

Shooting Efficiency

  • TS%: <50% (Poor), 50-55% (Average), 55-60% (Good), >60% (Elite)
  • eFG%: <45% (Poor), 45-50% (Average), 50-55% (Good), >55% (Elite)
  • 3P%: <30% (Poor), 30-35% (Average), 35-40% (Good), >40% (Elite)

Scoring

  • PPP: <0.8 (Inefficient), 0.8-1.0 (Average), 1.0-1.2 (Good), >1.2 (Elite)
  • ORtg: <100 (Poor), 100-110 (Average), 110-120 (Good), >120 (Elite)

Playmaking

  • A/TO: <1.0 (Poor), 1.0-1.5 (Average), 1.5-2.5 (Good), >2.5 (Elite)
  • USG%: <15% (Low), 15-20% (Role Player), 20-27% (Secondary), >27% (Primary)

Game Score

  • GmSc: <5 (Below Average), 5-10 (Average), 10-15 (Good), 15-20 (Very Good), >20 (Dominant)
